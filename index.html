• Bio
    Biography

    Fighting Sparrow is a celtic-punk band based in Niš, Serbia; established in 2018.

    Our music style is a mixture of punk energy and traditional celtic music, where melodies of a tin whistle and banjo (or mandolin or irish bouzuki) cut through the heavy guitar riffs, and all that followed by lyrics that describe everyday ups and downs of a man.

    By now we had plenty of performances in Serbia and Bulgaria and we're preparing our debut album.

    Also, we released a single with an official video as well, called „Drink it“ , which is available on our YouTube channel.
